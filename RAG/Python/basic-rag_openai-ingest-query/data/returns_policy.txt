RETURNS AND REFUNDS POLICY
Last updated: January 2025

SECTION 1: GENERAL RETURNS
We accept returns on most items within 30 days of the original purchase date.
Items must be in their original condition, unused, and with all original packaging
and tags attached. Proof of purchase is required for all returns.

SECTION 2: HOW TO INITIATE A RETURN
To start a return, contact our support team at support@example.com or call
1-800-555-0100 during business hours (Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm).
You will receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number within
1 business day. Ship the item back using the prepaid label we provide.

SECTION 3: SALE ITEMS
Items purchased on sale or with a discount code can be returned within
14 days of purchase. Sale items are eligible for store credit only,
not a cash refund.

SECTION 4: EXCHANGES
We are happy to exchange items for a different size or color, subject to
availability. Exchanges follow the same 30-day window as standard returns.
Contact support to arrange an exchange.

SECTION 5: REFUND PROCESSING
Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will process your refund
within 5 to 7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

SECTION 6: NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS
The following items cannot be returned:
- Digital downloads and software licenses
- Personalized or custom-made products
- Perishable goods
- Items marked as final sale at time of purchase
