# KnowYa AI Assistant: Your Intelligent Business Assistant

**Empower your website with an AI that truly understands your data.**

This is a comprehensive demo solution designed to help businesses turn their internal documents and datasets into a conversational experience. Instead of searching through folders or complex databases, your team and your customers can simply "ask" the AI.

---

## 🌟 What can KnowYa Assistant do for you?

KnowYa isn't just another chatbot. It is an **Intelligent Knowledge Assistant** that lives on your website and knows your business inside and out.

*   **Answer Complex Questions**: It reads your documents and provides accurate answers based *only* on the information you provide.
*   **Visualize Data**: Ask for a sales comparison or a summary, and KnowYa can generate **instant charts and reports** right in the chat.
*   **Fits Your Brand**: Comes with premium, modern designs (i.e. Emerald and Slate) that look professional and high-end on any website.
*   **Safe and Secure**: It uses "Shadow DOM" technology, which is a fancy way of saying it won't break your website's design when you install it.

---

## 🚀 Choose Your Experience

This demo offers two ways to interact with the AI, depending on your needs:

### 1. The Knowledge Assistant
**Perfect for: Customer Support, FAQs, and Document Research.**
This version focuses on your documents (PDFs, manuals, company policies). It features a friendly, draggable robot icon. When users have a question, the robot finds the answer in your knowledge base.

### 2. The Data Assistant
**Perfect for: Business Dashboards, Operational Tools, and Sales Data.**
This version is more powerful. It connects directly to your business data (like reservations, inventory, or customer lists). It can analyze trends, compare numbers, and help you make decisions faster.

## 🛠️ How It Works (The Simple Version)

You don't need to be a developer to understand the process:

1.  **Feed the Brain**: You provide your documents or data files to the system.
2.  **AI Learning**: Our backend "indexes" this information so the AI can find exactly what it needs in milliseconds.
3.  **Drop & Go**: A single line of code is added to your website, and your assistant is live!

## 📋 High-Level Setup (For your Technical Team)

If you are ready to experience, here is the structure of this solution:

*   **`pgvector-search-kb`**: The "Search Engine." This handles the heavy lifting of storing and searching your data using advanced vector technology.
*   **`chatbot-widget`**: The "Face." This contains the embeddable chat window that your users see.
*   **`chatbot-customizable`**: The "Flexible Assistant." A version of the chatbot that can be configurable and run with or without a database, perfect for quick demos or specific business needs.

*For detailed installation steps, please refer to the ReadMe files inside each folder.*
